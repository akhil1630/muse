THE BESPOKE FLOW

How Customisation Works

01

Share your idea

Share your color scheme, sizes, and reference motifs. We understand your design aesthetic and home context.

02

Discuss your design

We sketch configurations, finalize lettering, draft layouts, and select perfect dried flowers or crystals together.

03

Handcrafted with care

Artisanal layers of high-grade clear resin are hand-poured, slow-cured, gilded, and hand-polished to a liquid glass sheen.

04

Delivered with love

Each creation is hand-wrapped in signature premium linen storage box and courier shipped with protective tracking.
